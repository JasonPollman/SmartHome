Help Topics

Accounts

Adding User Accounts

  1. From the login page, enter a valid email address and secure password.
  2. Click "Register".
  3. That's it! Your account has been created and you will be automatically logged in.

I Forgot My Password

  1. From the login page, enter your email address in the username field.
  2. Click "Sign In".
  3. A link will appear that reads: "Send Password Reset"
  4. Click the link and a password reset email will be sent to your email address.
  5. Log in with the password provided in the email.
  6. Once logged in change your password immediately!

Devices

Connectivity

Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Make sure your smart devices are on the same network as the SmartHome API.
  2. Check that you have a valid internet connection.
  3. Refer to the device's instruction manual to ensure that your device is properly configured.
  4. Check the SmartHome API Terminal for any error messages.
  5. Open a ticket a the SmartHome Help Desk.

Configuring Devices

From the My Devices page, select a device you would like to configure.

Once you've selected a device, you'll be redirected to that device's page.

From there, you can modify each of the device's "widgets" by moving its sliders or clicking its buttons.

Schedules

Adding Schedules

From the Schedules page, click the icon to create a new schedule.

Editing Schedules

From the Schedules page, click on a schedule you would like to modify and you will be redirected to that specific schedules' settings page.

Schedule Settings

Here you can modify the generic settings of the schedule, such as its name and time.

Device Settings

Here you can change and modify the schedule's devices settings which will be changed when the schedule is enforced.

Deleting Schedules

From the Schedules page, click on the schedule you would like to delete and you will be redirected to that schedule's settings page. From there, click on the icon in the top right corner of the page.

Rules

Adding Rules

From the Rules page, click the icon to create a new rule.

Editing Rules

From the Rules page, click on a rule you would like to modify and you will be redirected to that specific rule's settings page.

Rule Settings

Here you can modify the generic settings of the rule, such as its name and source and target devices.

Source Device Settings

Here you can change and modify the source device (e.g. the device that triggers the rule).

Target Device Settings

Here you can change and modify the target device (e.g. the device that the rule acts upon).

Deleting Rules

From the Rules page, click on the rule you would like to delete. You will be redirected to that rule's setting page. From there, click on the icon in the top right corner of the page.
